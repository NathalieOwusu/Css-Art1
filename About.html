< About ME >

Hello, Im Nathalie.
I'm a software engineer with a passion for design, blending my technical skills with a love for creativity.
My design philosophy centers on breaking boundaries and coloring outside the lines, drawing inspiration from contemporary art to neo-expressionism, with Basquiat being my favorite artist.
My career goal is to work alongside other creatives to build a more vibrant and inclusive tech space. Beyond coding, I engage in graphic design, digital art, and nail design, which complement my technical work and allow me to approach problems with a unique artistic perspective.
My core values include a commitment to accessibility, user-centered design, and continuous learning, ensuring that my work remains innovative and inclusive.
